1. Introduction

Collarwork Design ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with applicable laws, including Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Quebec’s Law 25.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about our services. This may include your name, email address, and project specifications.

Your information is processed strictly for the purposes of evaluating project inquiries, responding to communications, and fulfilling contractual obligations.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Data is accessed solely by authorized personnel at Collarwork Design.

You have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data. You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us.
